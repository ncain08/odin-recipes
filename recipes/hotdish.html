Tater Tot Hot Dish

Minnesota Tater Tot Hot Dish

Description

So we call this classic comfort food Tater Tot Hotdish while many of you may call it Tater Tot Casserole. Whatever the name you use, this is a family favorite and a classic comfort food here in Minnesota!

For those of you who don't know, tater tot hotdish is a delectable dish made with corn, peas, carrots, green beans, and other veggies. Tater tot hotdish also has ground meat, cream of mushroom soup (some like to use cream of celery, instead), and tater tots on top.

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs grouind beef
  • 2 cans cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 cup corn
  • 1 cup peas and carrots mixed veggies
  • 1 package tater tots
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Put the diced onion in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the ground beef and saute until ground beef is cooked and onions are translucent.
  3. Grease a 9x13 pan.
  4. Pour the cooked meat and onions into the 9x13 pan. Spread the veggies evenly over the top. Add the cheddar cheese. Spread the cream of mushroom soup over the veggies. Some people mix it here, some people don't. It's up to you.
  5. Place the tater tots evenly on top of the mixture in straight lines.
  6. Bake for 1 hour. Check. If tater tots are not golden brown and the veggies are still crunchy, put in over for another 20 minutes.
  7. Let cool and enjoy!
